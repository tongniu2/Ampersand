2 Days. 12+ Speakers. 4 Workshops.

Interested in hearing from CEOs and executives of top companies speak on a wide range of multidisciplinary topics? Join us March 25th and 26th at BASIC’s annual 2-day conference, Ampersand 2017: Innovation for the Social Good! Come hear about innovative wearable technologies that uses biosensory data to enhance performance. Learn about how virtual reality (VR) is changing the way we create art. Discover new sustainability initiatives being implemented both in Canada and abroad!

For just $15, you’ll get access to over 12 keynote speakers, 4 exciting workshops, an exclusive networking cocktail and more!


Conference Details:
Day 1 - Saturday, March 25 from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m. - McConnell 1st Floor
Networking Cocktail - Saturday, March 25 from 5p.m. to 7p.m. - Thomson House Ballroom
Day 2 - Sunday, March 26 from 10:30 a.m. to 4 p.m. - SSMU Ballroom.
Breakfast and lunch will be provided on both days.

Conference Topics

This year, we've invited VIP speakers from a number of interdisciplinary fields to discuss new innovative ideas and products. These speakers are leaders in their fields and visionaries who can provide key insight into trends of the future. See below for the topics we will be exploring and click the icons to discover related workshops we will be offering to further expand on that topic.

Wearables: Melding Machine Learning and BioTech

Learn how companies are using machine learning to leverage biodata and create wearable technology that improves health and wellbeing.

Innovations in Sustainability: Using the Future to Save the Present

Discover key innovations in sustainability at both the local and global scene.

Techmunity: Implications of Technology on Today's Communities

Explore the ethical implications of technology in today's society and the impacts it can have in shaping our futures.

Sharing Economies in Today's Expanding Startup Culture

Hear from top executives of some of Montreal's coolest startups on how they're leveraging sharing economies to create products that are by the people, for the people.

Digital Art: Technology in the Creative Process

Learn how artists and designers are incorporating virtual reality into the creative process.
