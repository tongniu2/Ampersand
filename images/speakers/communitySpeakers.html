Phillippe Beaudoin - VP Research Group at Element AI

Twenty years of professional experience as a software team manager, academic researcher and software developer, including 5 years at Google, Phillippe Beaudoin has a M. Sc., Ph. D. and Postdoc studies in Computer Science. He is a researcher focusing on bipedal control, machine learning and physics simulation.

He has published 20 significant scientific papers, patents or book chapters and is the founder of three corporations, including Element AI and ArcBees Consulting. He is a previous speaker at Google I/O 2011, and speaker in many public events, as well as a community leader on 3 open source projects and of a world-wide academic workgroup.
